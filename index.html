About SerenityOS

What is SerenityOS?

SerenityOS is a fully functional web-based operating system built with vanilla JavaScript. It demonstrates modern web development techniques by combining HTML5, CSS3, and pure JavaScript to create an interactive desktop environment with draggable windows, multiple applications, and a premium minimalist design.

Documentation

Getting Started

  1. Enter your name in the form
  2. Click "Launch OS" to access the desktop
  3. Click any app button in the taskbar to open it
  4. Drag windows by the header to move them
  5. Click × to close windows

Feature Guide

🧮 Calculator

Full-featured calculator with support for +, -, ×, ÷, decimal, and equals. Try: 5 + 3 = (shows 8)

⏱️ Timer

25-minute Pomodoro timer. Click Start to begin, Pause to stop, Reset to return to 25:00. Get an alert when finished.

✓ To-Do

Add tasks by typing and pressing Enter. Delete tasks with the × button. Your tasks persist even after closing.

📝 Notes

Create notes with title and content. Click "Save Note" to persist them. Notes stay saved between sessions.

✉️ Contact

Send a message by filling in your email and message. You'll see a success confirmation after sending.

💼 Portfolio & Projects

View your work history, projects, and contact information all in one organized interface.

Tips & Tricks

  • Drag Windows: Click and drag the header to move windows around
  • Layer Windows: The last clicked window appears on top
  • Persistent Data: Notes and To-Do items are saved in your browser
  • Real-time Clock: The topbar updates every second
  • Personalization: Your name appears in the greeting
